Understanding the Pareto Chart

Purpose: The Pareto chart helps identify the "vital few" causes that contribute to most of your downtime problems (80/20 rule).

  • Red bars: These are your highest impact causes - focus here for maximum improvement
  • Blue line: Shows cumulative percentage - helps identify the 80% threshold
  • 80% Rule: Typically, 20% of causes create 80% of the problems
  • Action Plan: Address causes on the left first for the biggest impact on reducing total downtime
